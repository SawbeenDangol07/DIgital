Refund Policy

Last updated: October 2026

1. Overview

Due to the nature of digital goods, all sales are final once the product key or digital asset has been accessed or downloaded. We cannot revoke a digital key once it has been revealed.

2. Exceptional Circumstances

We may offer refunds or replacements under the following exceptional circumstances:

  • The digital key or account provided is invalid or already used (must be reported within 24 hours of purchase).
  • The wrong product was delivered.
  • Duplicate transactions occurred.

3. How to Request a Refund

If you believe you are eligible for a refund based on the criteria above, please contact our support team at support@d-goods.com within 3 days of purchase with your order ID and proof of the issue.
