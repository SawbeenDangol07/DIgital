1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create or modify your account, request on-demand services, contact customer support, or otherwise communicate with us. This information may include: name, email, phone number, and payment information.

2. Use of Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • Process transactions and send related information.
  • Send support and administrative messages.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not share personal information with companies, organizations, and individuals outside of Digital Goods Marketplace unless one of the following circumstances applies:

  • With your consent.
  • For legal reasons.
  • With trusted third-party payment processors (e.g., Stripe, PayPal).
